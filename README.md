# Saudi Imprint

Saudi Imprint is an e-tourism platform designed to promote tourism within Saudi Arabia by providing a comprehensive digital experience for tourists. The platform showcases four main regions, each presented with images and detailed descriptions of the most popular tourist destinations.

## Features

* Browse and explore tourist destinations across four main regions in Saudi Arabia.
* View detailed information and images for popular attractions.
* Search and select licensed tour guides based on preferences.
* Book guided trips directly through the platform.
* Communicate with tour guides for inquiries before and during the trip.
* Secure online payment for booking tour guide services.
* Rate and review tour guides after completing trips to improve service quality.

## Purpose

The platform aims to enhance the tourism experience by simplifying trip planning, improving accessibility to local tour guides, and supporting the digital transformation of the tourism sector in Saudi Arabia.
